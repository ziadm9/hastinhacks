East Bay Hacks

What is East Bay Hacks?

East Bay Hacks is an in person high school hackathon happening on October 16-17, 2024. We bring together students from diverse backgrounds to collaborate, innovate, and solve real-world challenges through technology. Over the course of the event, participants will team up to design, code, and present their projects, gaining hands-on experience and valuable skills in programming, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Why sponsor?

Sponsoring East Bay Hacks offers a unique opportunity to connect with the next generation of tech innovators and leaders. By supporting us, you gain access to a diverse pool of young talent eager to showcase their skills and creativity. Your sponsorship will not only enhance your company’s visibility among future engineers and tech professionals but also demonstrate your commitment to fostering innovation and education in the technology sector.

If you are interested in parterning with us, please reach out at sponsor@eastbayhacks.com!
